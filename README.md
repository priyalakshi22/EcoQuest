## 🎮 About the Game

**EcoQuest** is more than just a survival game—it's a narrative-driven experience about redemption, responsibility, and the lasting consequences of human actions on the environment. Set in an ecologically devastated world, players must navigate through four distinct levels, each offering unique gameplay mechanics and environmental challenges.

### 🌟 Key Features

- **4 Immersive Levels**: From contaminated laboratories to mystical dream worlds and tribal villages
- **Multiple Gameplay Mechanics**: Stealth, combat, puzzle-solving, and resource management
- **Environmental Message**: Real-world themes of sustainability and ecological responsibility
- **Rich Narrative**: Follow a scientist's journey to redemption after unleashing a viral outbreak
- **Strategic Gameplay**: Balance resource management with survival instincts
- **360° Exploration**: Full camera control and open terrain navigation

## 🎯 Game Objectives

### Level 1: Laboratory Escape
Escape from a contaminated forest research facility using stealth-based mechanics. Navigate through the collapsing, infected lab while learning the consequences of scientific negligence.

### Level 2: Dream World
Explore an otherworldly realm representing the protagonist's subconscious. Solve memory-based puzzles and confront symbolic landscapes that represent inner conflict.

### Level 3: Tribal Village
Arrive at a sustainable community that has developed eco-friendly methods to combat the zombie epidemic. Build trust, learn their practices, and cooperate to prevent further outbreaks.

### Level 4: Jungle Exploration
Venture into the borderland jungle to gather resources, fight infected enemies, and uncover hidden environmental mysteries related to the virus's origin.

## 🚀 Development Process

The project was developed using **Agile methodology** over multiple sprints:

1. **Planning**: High-level task prioritization at the start of each sprint
2. **Development**: Systematic implementation of game mechanics and levels
3. **Testing**: Continuous bug detection and usability improvements
4. **Review**: End-of-sprint feedback and retrospectives
5. **Iteration**: Process improvements based on lessons learned

### Challenges Overcome
- ✅ Tight deadline management through core feature prioritization
- ✅ Unity bugs resolved through frequent testing
- ✅ Team coordination during member illness via Trello and real-time collaboration
- ✅ Data loss prevention through regular backups
